What's inside

Everything an affiliate needs.
Nothing they don't.

AffiliIQ is designed around how affiliates actually work — not how sellers think you do.

📊
Commission Goal Calculator
Set your monthly commission goal and instantly see how many videos you need to post — based on your own historical performance data, with best, average, and worst case projections.
🎬
Video Analytics Intelligence
Rank every video you've posted by commission, views, CTR, or conversion rate. Spot your best performers and understand what's driving revenue with AI-powered insights.
🔗
Ad Code Manager
Track all your active affiliate links and ad codes across every shop. Export codes, draft professional outreach messages to brands, and never miss an expiry again.
💰
Financial Dashboard
See your full withdrawal history, track your orders and samples, monitor commission lock dates, and export your financial data for reporting — with full date range filtering.
⚠️
Dead Link Detection
Automatically identify videos with broken affiliate links — whether the product is out of stock, delisted, or expired. See the revenue at risk and find replacement products instantly.
📈
Performance Archive
View your full month-by-month performance history with interactive charts. Filter by date range, track trends, and add historical data manually for periods before you connected TikTok.
